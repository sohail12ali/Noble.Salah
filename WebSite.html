Feature Overview

1 Prayer Times & Alerts

Get accurate prayer times based on your location with multiple calculation methods (Umm al-Qura, ISNA, MWL, etc.). Receive customizable notifications and countdowns to each prayer.

2 Ramadan Special Features

Special Ramadan mode with Sehri/Iftar alerts, countdown timers, and a dedicated timetable. The app automatically switches to Ramadan-themed colors during the holy month.

3 Qibla Compass

Precise Qibla direction finder with magnetic field detection and compass error correction. Works anywhere in the world with clear visual indicators.

4 Quran & Islamic Resources

Complete Quran with Arabic text and translations in multiple languages. Includes daily duas, 99 Names of Allah, Six Kalimas, and educational content about the Five Pillars of Islam.

5 Community Features

Find nearby mosques and halal restaurants, ask questions to scholars, stay updated with Islamic news, and connect with local Islamic businesses and services.

6 Personalization

Customize your experience with multiple language support (English, Arabic, Urdu, etc.), theme options (day/night/Ramadan), and personalized prayer notifications.
